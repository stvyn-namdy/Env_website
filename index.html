What does Environmental Science entail?

Environmental science is the study of the effects of natural and unnatural processes, and of interactions of the physical components of the planet on the environment. is an unusual academic discipline in that it requires scientific knowledge about the natural world, as well as an understanding about ways in which humans interact with the natural world. We examine effects of human actions on the environment, and the means by which policies, regulations, and decisions influence human actions.
